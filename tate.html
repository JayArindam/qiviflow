About

QIVIFLOW is a leading provider of Pharmacovigilance data, specifically focusing on adverse drug reactions for the healthcare sector. Our goal is to establish smart connections that deliver innovative solutions and actionable insights to enhance human health while minimizing negative effects. We are dedicated to stimulating the reporting of adverse drug reactions (ADR) and medical feedback, in order to create a safer and healthier environment for everyone. Our cutting-edge technology and expert team work together to deliver the highest quality data and insights, allowing healthcare professionals to make informed decisions and ultimately improve patient outcomes.
